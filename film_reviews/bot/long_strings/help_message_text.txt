Hello and welcome to the FilmReviewer telegram bot.

Using this bot you can leave your review on the film you have watched or read other people's reviews on the films you would like to watch.

To read the reviews on the film you would like to watch, use /read_reviews <film name>.

To leave a review on the film you have watched, use /leave_review <film name>.

NOTE: You can use any case for the film name (e.g. American Psycho = american psycho).

If you experience any issues with the bot or find any bugs, use /report_bug or DM @cat_dev_lol if you feel like it needs to be fixed immediately.
Use /changelog to see the bot's current version and changelog.
